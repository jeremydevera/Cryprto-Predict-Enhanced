Reality check: v3 vs v2 — barely any improvement

  • What you gained: ATR Mult 4.5 → flip 1 bar earlier; HTF Slope min loosened → confirms ~0.5 bar earlier.
  • What you gave back: KDE VA Max Density 0.3 → 0.2 → location gate now waits ~1 bar longer; DI Period 16 → 25 → direction confirmation lags ~1–2 bars longer.
  • Net: v3 entry lands roughly where v2 did (~bar 22–23). You're moving sideways, not closer to bar 17.

The pattern: every time you loosen one gate (good), you tighten another (cancels it). To actually shift entries earlier, you have to keep the loosening AND stop adding new strict requirements. The relentless tightening of KDE VA across v1→v2→v3 (0.4 → 0.3 → 0.2) is the main thing blocking progress.

Three things to try in v4:
1. KDE VA Max Density 0.2 → 0.5, keep LB 60, BW 0.2 (sharp curve, loose threshold)
2. Disable Impulse Candle Gate entirely
3. DI Period 25 → 14 (classic Wilder)
