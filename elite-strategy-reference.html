Elite Context Breakout Strategy Reference

CryptoPredict Terminal  ·  Generated 2026-04-25  ·  Open in Chrome → Ctrl+P → Save as PDF
Overview
Concept: Identifies a swing high/low preceded by tight consolidation, waits for a high-quality breakout candle with volume surge and strong close, then optionally a retest of the broken level, and enters in the direction of the break. Every signal must align with the higher timeframe trend structure.

Always-On Hard Gates (not toggleable)
  1. LOW vol regime — no signal fires regardless of setup quality
  2. Price position — must be above swing (buy) or below swing (sell); price must have broken out
  3. Fresh or retested — stale unretested breaks blocked (break must be ≤ freshness limit bars old, or retest must have occurred)
  4. Liquidity pool — MEDIUM vol A-grade only: cluster of prior swing highs/lows within 0.2% of the swing level must exist
  5. HTF Structure — HH+HL for bull, LH+LL for bear; derived from HTF candles
  6. Consolidation range — 8-bar range must be within ATR threshold
  7. Consolidation duration — at least 5 of 8 bars individually tight (≤ 0.8× ATR each)
  8. Grade ≥ 1 — invalid grade (0) always blocked
  9. B-grade + HTF EMA50 disagrees — B-grade counter-EMA setups blocked
  10. B-grade + no retest — B-grade fresh entries blocked; must wait for retest
  11. Retest EMA20 alignment — if retest occurred, wick must be within 0.3% of EMA20
  12. RSI gate — MEDIUM A-grade: ≥55/≤45 · MEDIUM B-grade: ≥52/≤48 · others: ≥50/≤50
